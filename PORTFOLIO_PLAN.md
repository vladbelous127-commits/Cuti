# M.I Tech NG — Portfolio / Agency Website Plan

A premium AI consultancy website (not a personal portfolio). Positioning:
**M.I Tech NG** — an AI-focused technology company. The founder, Mustapha
Ibrahim, is presented as the face of the company, but the company builds and
delivers the work.

## Design System

- **Theme:** Dark luxury.
- **Colors:**
  - Background: near-black `#0A0A0A` / `#050505`
  - Brand green (accent): `#74A432`
  - Gold (highlight): `#C9A227`
  - Foreground / text: off-white `#EDEDED`, muted `#A1A1A1`
- **Font:** Poppins (Google Fonts) across the site.
- **Feel:** generous spacing, subtle gradients, glow accents, glass cards,
  smooth scroll-reveal animations, fully responsive.
