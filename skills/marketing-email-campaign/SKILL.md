# Marketing Email Campaign

Produce campaign-ready email copy plus the plan around it: audience, sequence, timing, and success metrics. A single email without a goal and a follow-up plan usually underperforms, so always deliver the campaign context even when the user asks for just one email.

## Workflow

1. **Pin down the campaign basics.** Identify from the conversation (ask only if truly missing): product/offer, audience segment, single primary goal (clicks, replies, purchases, sign-ups), and the one action each email should drive. One email = one call to action.
2. **Choose the campaign shape.** Match the goal to a structure:
   - Announcement/launch → 3 emails: teaser, launch, last-chance
   - Nurture/drip → 4–6 emails: value, value, social proof, offer
   - Re-engagement → 2–3 emails: "we miss you", incentive, breakup email
   - Newsletter → recurring single email with fixed sections
3. **Write each email.** For every email produce: 3 subject-line options (under 50 characters, no clickbait caps), preview text (under 90 characters), body copy, and one clear CTA button label. Keep body copy under 200 words unless the user's brand voice calls for long-form.
4. **Add the send plan.** Recommend day/time spacing between emails and the trigger or segment for each send.
5. **Define success.** State the metric to watch per email (open rate for subject tests, CTR for body tests, conversion for the sequence) and a realistic benchmark for the user's industry if known.

## Output format

For each email in the sequence:

```
### Email N — [purpose] (send: [timing/trigger])
Subject options:
1. ...
2. ...
3. ...
Preview text: ...
---
[Body copy]
---
CTA: [button label] → [destination]
```

Close with a short "Send plan" table (email, trigger/day, segment, metric to watch).

## Quality bar

- Write like a person, not a brand robot. Contractions are fine. Exclamation points are rationed to one per email, maximum.
- The first sentence must earn the second — never open with "We're excited to announce."
- Every claim needs a concrete detail (number, name, date) or it gets cut.
- Include an unsubscribe reminder note for compliance (CAN-SPAM/GDPR) when generating full campaigns.
